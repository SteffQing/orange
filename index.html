Distant Finance is building an ecosystem that bridges the void between NFTs and the MetaVerse to their application in the real world.

Distant Ecosystem

lending

NFTs are the new Gold. They have been for the better parts of 2022. However, they are still limited to aesthetics, staking and P2E.
Changing the narratives is our rationale to building the first NFT Lending Market on KCC where owners can put up their NFTs for leverage to take loans and the funders earn a fixed returns for the duration in which the loan was given out.

Learn More

game guild

Play-to-Earn Games makes heavy use of InGame NFTs and so due, access might be limited based on cost factors.
We simplify the accessibility by leveraging smart contracts and a trustless system to lend out NFTs to Gamers according to a set revenue sharing economics.
Distant is also pioneering a PIE model (Play, Interact, Earn) via a range of varied sub-products embedded in the Game-Guild.

Learn More

launchpad

The perfect launch platform for any Game developer or Artist to offer their NFT work to a decentralized community! Facilitating every launch on the platform follows a set of rules and approval from the Distant DAO: The Quango.

bridge

NFTs on Ethereum, BSC or even Solana! Any NFT on an L1, L2 or a rollup chain can get bridged to KCC.
Distant Finance is building and integrating a secure Bridge network that allows you to move your NFTs around, there has never been a more frictionless transition into and out of KCC.

marketplace

In order to cater to a more diverse group of KCC enthusiasts, Distant Marketplace has positioned itself as a Hub for both new and validated collections. A decentralized market that is open to participation from anyone and everyone.
